MODE OF OPERATION

Private Public Partnerships
The company has programmed to develop a Public Private Partnerships in the management of incoming/outgoing Armed Merchant Vessels at the port of Mombasa and the entire Kenyan Coastline.
To this end it is imperative MESL has partnered with the following Institutions for smooth delivery of its mandate:-

  • The Kenya Maritime Authority – Obtain information on all incoming Merchant Ships with Armed Guards Onboard through their local Agents.
  • The Kenya Firearms Bureau - Convenience in issuing Import/Export and Temporary Firearm well before the ship arrives at the port for the sake of verification.
  • The Immigration - Obtain Temporary Permits and visas for the visiting and transit crew onboard, well before the ships arrival and subsequent docking.
  • The NIS - Gather information on the composition of the crew, illegal immigrants disembarking in the high seas before the ship’s docking, number and type of weapons entering in the country undeclared.
  • The Kenya Police Service - Safe storage of weapons in the Police Armoury at the Kilindini Port and law enforcement where there is culpability by a visiting Vessel.
  • The KRA and Customs Department - Verification of weapons onboard the vessel and enforcement of payments to be made to the exchequer.
  • The Coast Guard- Boarding the Vessels in the High seas to verify the actual number of weapons onboard is what has been declared by the Local Agents.
