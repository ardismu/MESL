WHO ARE WE?

Marine Escort Services Limited is a limited liability company incorporated under the companies Act Chapter 486 of the laws of Kenya. The company is owned by a group of senior retired military officers from the three branches of Kenya Defense Forces, namely the Kenya Army, the Kenya Air force and the Kenya Navy. Other members of the disciplined forces, namely the Kenya Police Service, the National Youth Service and other security agencies are co-opted members.
The company was registered to harness the knowledge and talent that otherwise was wasting away due to early retirement owing to the fact that the Kenya Defense Forces retirement system is based on a fixed age limit pegged on a rank-to-age limitation.

Marine Escort Services is now converting to an integrated security solutions company and has embraced all uniformed personnel with a specific special interest with all former uniformed and retired NIS personnel. All that notwithstanding, we are committed to the vastness of our professional expertise and the need to exploit the economies of scale, exigencies of administration, operational and logistical advantages attendant to bringing on board individuals and entities with the capacity to deepen and widen the length and breadth of the reach of the company.
